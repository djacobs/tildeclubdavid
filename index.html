Top 4 Unix Tips

  • #nodrama. When someone has an opinion about vi or emacs, end the conversation as quickly as possible. Like your favorite sports team or Harry Potter house allegiance, the editor chooses you, not the other way around.
  • Learn about the pipe. When you understand the pipe, you understand everything
  • Learn up on ssh keys [PDF]
  • Craig Hockenberry recently wrote up a great guide to the terminal. It got a lot of attention in the Apple community (folks who follow Craig) but his old school approach is appreciated by the author and is sympatico with the quickly emerging ~ way of doing things. If you are an apple person, you may also appreciate that you can practice his tips on your desktop first.
